How to Play

  • 🎯 You and your opponent each have 3 hearts. Drop a foe to 0 to win.
  • 🃏 Every card is a weapon. Build a deck of exactly 6; you hold 3 and cycle the next one in after each play.
  • 🔵 You have 5 mana, regaining +1 every round. Each card costs mana — Hook is free.
  • 🗿 Drag a card onto your slot — or tap it to lift it, then tap the slot to place it. (Keys 1/2/3 lift, Enter places.)
  • ⚔️➡️🗡️ Projectile beats Melee · Melee beats Katana · Katana blocks Projectiles (cuts back ½❤).
  • 🔄 Uno Reverse deflects a projectile back at the enemy. 🛡️ Shield (always free, key S) blocks attacks but is your whole turn.
  • 🪝 Hook (free) smashes a Shield and disables it 1 turn — but loses to any weapon. Harpoon / R-Peg are projectiles that also disable a Shield for 2 / 3 turns.
  • 🤝 Same-type clashes are a stalemate: both weapons still land, for ½ heart less.
